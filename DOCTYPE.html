Privacy Policy

TRIXO TRIVIA BATTLES

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our players. TRIXO Trivia Battles is a guest-only application that requires no account creation, login, or personal data entry.

2. Local Data Storage

All game progress data (coins, levels, themes, usernames) is stored locally on your device's storage. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not sent to our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

3. Online Multiplayer Data

When playing online matches:

  • Your username and game score are stored on our servers
  • This data is used only for gameplay and leaderboard ranking
  • We do not store email addresses or personal information
  • We do not use tracking or analytics cookies

4. Advertisements

Our app displays:

  • Interstitial ads between matches
  • These ads are served by third-party ad networks
  • Ad networks may collect limited data as per their privacy policies
  • You can close ads at any time

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

TRIXO Trivia Battles does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no features that require or collect personal information from minors. If we learn we've inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

6. Data Retention

  • Local device data: Stored until you uninstall the app
  • Online leaderboard data: Retained for competitive ranking purposes
  • We retain no historical purchase or transaction data

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect online multiplayer data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your leaderboard data
  • Request deletion of your online profile
  • Contact us with questions

9. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy or data practices, contact: support@example.com
